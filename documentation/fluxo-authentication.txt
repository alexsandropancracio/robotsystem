LOGIN
 └─► access_token (JWT curto)
 └─► refresh_token (longo, persistido no banco)

REQUEST AUTENTICADA
 └─► Authorization: Bearer <access_token>
 └─► deps/auth.py
 └─► get_current_user
 └─► usuário válido
 └─► endpoint (/me, /protected, etc)

TOKEN EXPIRADO
 └─► refresh_token
 └─► rotate_refresh_token
 └─► novo access_token
 └─► novo refresh_token
